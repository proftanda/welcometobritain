About.

Developed over a 24 hour workshop, Welcome To Britain aimed to draw on the visual language and mechanics of Minecraft to find a format for making the news engaging for younger children.

The project focused on the topic of migration and places the user as a protagonist in an attempted journey to Britain, along the way, linking to related news stories about events in similar journeys that have actually taken place.

A prototype using Minecraft or a Minecraft-like web-platform such as voxel.js proved too complex for the time so this prototype was developed using Twine as a simple way to map out the story and to deliver the interaction, with the intention of overlaying this interaction on Minecraft videos or images.
